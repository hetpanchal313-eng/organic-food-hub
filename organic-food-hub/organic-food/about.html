Who We Are

Started with a Simple Belief

Organic Food Hub was founded in 2020 with a simple but powerful belief — everyone deserves access to fresh, healthy, chemical-free food. We started as a small initiative connecting city families with organic farmers, and today we serve over 12,000 happy households across India.

We work directly with 50+ certified organic farms to bring you the freshest produce, harvested and delivered within 24 hours. No middlemen, no warehouses, no compromises.
